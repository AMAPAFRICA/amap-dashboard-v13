Safeguards & Data Ethics

AMAP data governance · POPIA · SADC Model Law · Master Profile Template v5

✓
All cases are validatedEvery incident on this platform has been reviewed by SARW, de-duplicated, and verified against at least two independent sources before publication.

What appears on this map

Only cases reviewed by SARW, de-duplicated, and validated against at least two sources are plotted. All other submissions are held in the internal review queue. Duplicate submissions are merged into a single canonical record.

Data Protection

No personal names, IDs, health records, or community contact details appear here. Sensitive data is held in separate, password-protected files referenced by case code only. POPIA and SADC Model Law compliance is mandatory for all monitors.

HRD Protection

Monitor identities are protected where there is any risk of retaliation. The CASA programme provides SLAPP litigation support. Community reporters in high-risk contexts are covered under the AMAP HRD protocol.
